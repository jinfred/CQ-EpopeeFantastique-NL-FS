Celtic Impulse

Genre: World Collection: Celtic and Folk 
Time: 2:38 144 BPM (Allegro - Pretty Fast) 
Instruments: Hammer Dulcimer, Harp, Bouzouki, Tin Whistle 

Simple, easy-to-play folk-influenced tune in the D Dorian mode (related to D Minor).

Dark, Driving, Mystical
